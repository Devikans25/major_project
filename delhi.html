Welcome to New Delhi - The Heart of India

New Delhi, the capital of India, is a vibrant city blending history and modernity. It is home to iconic landmarks, bustling markets, and delicious street food.

India Gate

A war memorial honoring soldiers, India Gate is a must-visit landmark in New Delhi.

Qutub Minar

The tallest brick minaret in the world, Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Red Fort

A symbol of India's rich history, the Red Fort was the main residence of Mughal emperors.

Lotus Temple

A beautiful Bahá'í House of Worship, known for its stunning lotus-shaped architecture.
