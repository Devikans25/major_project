Welcome to Hyderabad - The City of Pearls

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is a vibrant city known for its historic monuments, delicious Hyderabadi biryani, and bustling bazaars. Explore the city’s rich cultural heritage and modern attractions.

Charminar

The iconic landmark of Hyderabad, built in 1591, known for its stunning architecture.

Golconda Fort

A magnificent fortress with an intriguing past and an excellent sound and light show.

Hussain Sagar Lake

A picturesque lake with a massive Buddha statue at the center.

Ramoji Film City

One of the world’s largest film studio complexes, offering entertainment and tours.
