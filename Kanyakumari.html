Kanyakumari, the southernmost tip of India, is famous for its breathtaking beaches, the iconic Vivekananda Rock Memorial, and its mesmerizing sunrise and sunset views. A place where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean meet.

Vivekananda Rock Memorial

A spiritual site dedicated to Swami Vivekananda, offering stunning sea views.

Thiruvalluvar Statue

A towering 133-feet statue honoring the famous Tamil poet and philosopher.

Kanyakumari Beach

Famous for its spectacular sunrise and sunset views over the ocean.

Padmanabhapuram Palace

An ancient wooden palace showcasing Kerala's traditional architecture.
